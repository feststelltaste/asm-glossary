# Multi-Step Planning

> The ability of an agent to decompose a complex goal into a sequence of concrete subtasks and execute them step by step, without requiring a human to specify each step explicitly. The quality of planning determines how far an agent can operate autonomously before needing correction. Poor planning leads to compounding errors; good planning is what makes the agentic loop productive over longer tasks.
