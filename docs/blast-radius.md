# Blast Radius

> The scope of potential impact when an agent makes a change to a codebase. A small blast radius means a modification affects only a narrow, well-isolated part of the system; a large blast radius means ripple effects can reach distant modules, services, or teams. Controlling blast radius is a core concern in agent-assisted modernization and motivates patterns like slicing, seams, and incremental improvement.
