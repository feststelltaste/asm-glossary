# About

This glossary is a personal collection of terms by [Markus Harrer](https://markusharrer.de), assembled to establish a shared vocabulary for talking about the use of AI agents in software modernization projects.

The terms cover the full arc of agent-assisted modernization: from the fundamentals of how agents reason and act, through the infrastructure that makes them work in real codebases, to the debt concepts, transformation patterns, safety mechanisms, and human oversight practices that make the approach sustainable.

The goal is not completeness but precision: a compact set of terms that makes conversations about agentic modernization less ambiguous.
