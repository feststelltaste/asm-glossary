# Intent Engineering

> The deliberate practice of translating goals, constraints, decision rationale, and domain knowledge into machine-readable and human-understandable artifacts before an agent begins work. Includes creating and maintaining ADRs, specifications, domain models, acceptance criteria, and context documents. Intent engineering is the countermeasure to intent debt and ensures that both agent and human know why the system does what it does.
