# Guided AI

> A data-driven approach to focusing agent attention on the highest-value areas of a legacy codebase before modernization begins. Uses software mining techniques (change frequency analysis, complexity hotspot detection, coupling metrics) to identify which modules, classes, or components carry the most risk or offer the highest modernization return. Guides the agent's scope rather than having it discover the codebase blindly through agentic search. Distinct from agentic search in that it operates on aggregated historical and structural signals rather than navigating live code on demand.
